HOW TO ATTRACT AN AUDIENCE ON SOCIAL MEDIA THIS QUARANTINE?

search engine optimization | 29 June 2020

Every business strives to make sure people come across their product or service and not of their competitors. The company wants its products or services to be seen by their audience first and prefer their its competitors. Mindless Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and costly pay-per-click advertising is not the answer every time. There are plenty of ways to increase your traffic too organically. Here’s how:

1. Optimize for readers:

First, you need to understand your buyers’ persona and whom you will be addressing with your content. By creating quality content that resonates with your buyers, your SEO will naturally improve. This is where you need to tap into the main issues your buyers are facing and using appropriate keywords. Understanding your buyers’ persona will help with your SEO automatically.

2. Regular blogging:

The most effective way to increase your organic site traffic is by blogging regularly. You can deep dive into categories and create large helpful catalogs with persona-optimized content. These blogs help you center yourself in the market niche. However, poor writer, spammy, keyword-stuffed content can do more harm than good; therefore, avoid it at all costs.

3. Blogosphere:

Here’s where you read, comment, and link to other people’s sites and blogs, particularly those operating in your market. Here, other people can read, comment, and link to your business, thereby attracting more prospects. Quora is a neat place to start with where you can get your voice out by answering other people’s questions. You can provide valuable insights to people and get more prospects that way.

4. Long-tail keywords:

Going with the popular keywords won’t always work for you. Try to incorporate keywords that are more specific to your product or service. Google and other search engines will identify your website or blog as a destination for that particular subject, which in turn will boost your content in search rankings and help your ideal customers find you.
